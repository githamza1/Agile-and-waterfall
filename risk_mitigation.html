Risk Mitigation Strategies

Here, you will find strategies to mitigate the identified risks effectively.

Potential Mitigation Strategies:

  • Implement regular integration meetings among teams to identify compatibility issues early.
  • Conduct security audits for user authentication and payment processes.
  • Adopt agile methodologies to adapt to technical challenges promptly.
  • Allocate additional resources or adjust timelines if resource constraints are identified.
  • Engage stakeholders regularly to ensure alignment and address concerns quickly.
